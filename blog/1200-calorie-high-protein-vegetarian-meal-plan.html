1200‑Calorie High‑Protein Vegetarian Meal Plan for Weight Loss

Long‑tail keywords: 1200 calorie high protein vegetarian meal plan, Indian vegetarian weight loss plan protein, simple veg diet for fat loss.

One‑day sample

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt or low‑fat curd bowl + berries + chia (30g protein).
  • Lunch: Paneer/Tofu stir‑fry + mixed veggies + 1 small roti (35g protein).
  • Snack: Roasted chana or edamame + lemon (15g protein).
  • Dinner: Lentil + quinoa bowl with salad (30g protein).

Adjust calories up or down per your needs; consult a professional if unsure.

Grocery list

Paneer/tofu, lentils, chickpeas, quinoa, eggs (if ovo), yogurt/curd, leafy greens, colorful veg, berries, spices.

Tips

  • Distribute 25–35g protein per meal.
  • Prioritize fiber‑rich veg and hydration.
  • Pair with 7–9k+ steps and 2–3 strength sessions weekly.

Some adults add a non‑stimulant mitochondria support formula for daily energy.
