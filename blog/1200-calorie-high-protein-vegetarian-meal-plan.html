1200‑Calorie High‑Protein Vegetarian Meal Plan for Weight Loss

Long‑tail keywords: 1200 calorie high protein vegetarian meal plan, Indian vegetarian weight loss plan protein, simple veg diet for fat loss.

Who this plan is for

This sample day suits smaller adults aiming for gentle fat loss or anyone who prefers compact meals. If you’re more active or taller, increase portions by 10–30% or add a snack. Focus on protein and fiber first; then fit carbs/fats to preference.

One‑day sample (≈1200 kcal, 100–120 g protein)

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt/curd bowl (250 g) + berries + chia + 10–15 g nuts (30 g protein).
  • Lunch: Paneer/Tofu stir‑fry (150–180 g) + mixed veggies + 1 small roti or 1/2 cup rice (35–40 g).
  • Snack: Roasted chana or edamame (30–40 g) + lemon + salt (15–20 g).
  • Dinner: Lentil (dal) + quinoa bowl with salad; target 30 g protein total.

Adjust calories up or down per your needs; consult a professional if unsure.

2‑day rotation (simple variety)

  • Day A: as above.
  • Day B: Tofu scramble wrap; chickpea salad bowl; protein smoothie; veg chili with beans.

Grocery list (protein & staples)

Paneer/tofu, lentils, chickpeas, black beans, quinoa, oats, curd/yogurt, whey/plant protein, leafy greens, onions, tomatoes, colorful veg, berries, spices, olive/mustard oil, lemons.

Meal prep in 45 minutes

  1. Cook 2 cups lentils/beans; refrigerate portions.
  2. Batch roast mixed veggies; store in boxes.
  3. Chop salad base; keep dressing separate.
  4. Pre‑portion yogurt bowls and roasted chana.

Tips for staying full

  • Distribute 25–35 g protein per meal.
  • Include at least one high‑fiber fruit and two veg servings daily.
  • Drink a glass of water before meals; keep sodium moderate.
  • Pair plan with 7–10k steps/day and two to three strength sessions weekly.

Common vegetarian pitfalls

  • Relying on only carbs; add tofu/paneer/lentils to every plate.
  • Skipping fats entirely—use small amounts for taste and satiety.
  • Under‑salting and under‑hydrating in hot weather—leads to fatigue.
